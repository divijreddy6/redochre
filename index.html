Services

From early tests to final masters, we can join at any stage. Below is an overview of how we typically work.

Colour grading

Look development, show LUTs and final grading for features, series, docs and shorts. We’re comfortable working from offline timelines in a range of NLEs and camera formats.

  • Look sessions with directors and DPs
  • Hero frames & quick tests before shoot
  • SDR grading with HDR options on request

Online & finishing

Conform, clean-up and finishing passes aimed at making the picture feel invisible — in the best way. We keep changes documented so editorial and sound always know what has shifted.

  • Conform from offline to grading timelines
  • Minor VFX, beauty work & paint fixes
  • Text, titles & versioning for multiple deliveries

Mastering & delivery

Mastering for festivals, broadcast and platforms with an emphasis on clarity: you leave with a set of files and a delivery sheet that explain exactly what each version is for.

  • DCP for festival and theatrical screenings
  • Platform-ready packages and broadcast specs
  • Archival exports and frame-accurate reference files
